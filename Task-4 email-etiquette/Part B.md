## **Social Media Checklist for College Students**

###  **Do’s**

* Be respectful and polite in your posts and comments.  
* Think before you post—consider how it may affect your reputation.  
* Use privacy settings to control who can see your content.  
* Share accurate and verified information only.  
* Maintain a professional tone, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.

###  **Don’ts**

* Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, etc.).  
* Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.  
* Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.  
* Don’t spread rumors or fake news.  
* Don’t post anything that could harm your academic or career opportunities.
